Welcome to KnackArt Alley

Our history

For many years, Elay has been passionately pouring her creativity in transforming recycled materials into works of art, Junk Journals, at her home. Last year (2019), she decided to start her own business with her craft at Naga City (Philippines) in a cosy little studio near Ateneo de Naga University. Her works have been adored by the local Naga townsfolk, which has also been featured on the national television "Rated K"... Her art studio have became a success in no time, being recognized by the "International Association of Paper Crafters", and also a huge fanbase across the globe reflected by her instagram and youtube channel which has over a million subscribers..
